Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Text2Image. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide our services. When you use our text-to-image generator, we temporarily process:

  • The text prompts you provide
  • The images that are generated
  • Basic usage data to improve our service

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our basic services.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data collected to:

  • Generate images based on your text prompts
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Monitor and prevent abusive or inappropriate content
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of our service

4. Data Storage

All generated images are stored locally in your browser and are not automatically saved on our servers. You can download or clear these images at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We use advanced AI services for image generation. Your prompts are processed according to our partners' privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information about how they process data.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our website. These cookies do not collect personal information and are used solely for technical purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request restriction of processing of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
